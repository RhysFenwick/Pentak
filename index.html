Pentak

Popular among the sailors of Lhazaar, this game represents a naval battle between two fleets for control of an island chain.

Rules

Each player takes it in turn to move a piece. You can't move onto or over islands (dark green), or through other ships (even your own). Move onto your opponent's ships to sink them.

There are three ship types:
⛤ Flagship - Fast and powerful. Moves up to five hexes in a line.
⚔ Brig - Slow but strong. Moves one hex, but can't be sunk by a sloop.
⚜ Sloop - All-rounder. Moves up to two hexes in a line.

You can win one of two ways:
1. Wiping out your opponent's fleet, or
2. Having ships in 3 of the 4 island bays (light green) while your opponent has none.
